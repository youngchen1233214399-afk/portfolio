# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers and design leads reviewing Ziyang (Lucas) Chen's candidacy for AI innovation / AI experience design roles. They arrive from a CV link or a shared URL, skim on desktop first, and decide within a minute whether to open a project. Secondary audience: peers and collaborators.

## Product Purpose

A motion-led personal portfolio (ziyang-chen.vercel.app) that presents Ziyang's identity and four shipped prototypes (Eco-Bridge, ClarityBrief, 741, Bristopia). Success: a reviewer immediately reads the site itself as evidence of craft, previews a project, and clicks through to the live product or makes contact.

## Brand Personality

"Mineral Editorial": editorial, tactile, calm. Oversized serif type against mineral greens and greys; organic shapes on a strict grid. Self-assured rather than loud; experimental but easy to navigate; human-centred rather than generic AI-themed.

## Anti-references

- SaaS dashboard aesthetics, card-grid homepages, compressed case-study archives
- Generic AI blue/purple gradients, neon glow, glassmorphism, heavy blur, deep black shadows
- Glossy tech showreel vibes; endless marquees; custom cursors; decorative scroll prompts
- Copying the reference site's exact composition, texture or colour values

## Design Principles

- The site is a designed object first, a project directory second
- Contrast of organic material against systemic grid is the signature; protect it
- One lichen accent sitewide; project palettes live only inside project media
- Motion must communicate hierarchy, transition, feedback or spatial continuity, never fill space
- Large empty zones are composition, not waste

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast for body and control text; visible keyboard focus states with hover parity; no horizontal overflow at 390px; decorative layers are pointer-transparent; all non-essential motion collapses under prefers-reduced-motion; no custom cursor.

See VISUAL_STYLE.md for the full visual system (tokens, typography, shape language, motion rules); it serves as this project's DESIGN.md.
